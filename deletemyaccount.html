Key Information About Your Data

We collect personal information to provide job matching services. You can request account deletion at any time (see section 5). We retain some anonymized data for analytics purposes after account deletion.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Personal Data We Collect

When you use Jobs Finder, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, profile picture
  • Professional Data: Resume/CV, work history, education, skills
  • Job Preferences: Desired positions, salary range, location preferences
  • Activity Data: Job searches, applications, saved jobs

1.2 Automatic Data Collection

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Usage statistics and analytics
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized job recommendations
  • Match you with potential employers
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate important service updates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Sharing

We share information with:

  • Employers: When you apply for jobs, we share your application materials
  • Service Providers: Analytics, cloud storage, and customer support partners
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Account Deletion Request

You can request deletion of your account and personal data at any time:

  1. Open the Jobs Finder app and go to Settings
  2. Select "Account Settings"
  3. Choose "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm your request
  5. Alternatively, email us at [support email] with subject "Account Deletion Request"

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Data We Delete:

  • Your personal profile information
  • Saved resumes and application materials
  • Job search preferences and history
  • Account credentials

Data We Retain:

  • Anonymized usage data for analytics
  • Records of job applications already submitted to employers
  • Financial records required by law (if applicable)

Retention Period: Deletion occurs within 30 days of request. Some anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and access controls. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

7. Children's Privacy

Jobs Finder is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
